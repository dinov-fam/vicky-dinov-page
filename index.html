About Me

I'm an ORISE Science and Technology Fellow at U.S. Department of Energy - Solar Energy Technology Office. Previously, I was a UC Berkeley Regents' and Chancellor's Scholar.

I am passionate about renewable energy technologies and how we can best promote an energy world that ubiquitously provides affordable, reliable, and secure clean energy. I plan to leverage my technoeconomic and data skills to generate high impact solutions in the energy technology and policy spaces. I am excited and open to any and all opportunities in sustainability, energy systems, climate technology, and renewables.
